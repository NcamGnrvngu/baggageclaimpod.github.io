Baggage Claim

an unofficial Jet Lag: The Game companion podcast

Welcome to Baggage Claim, an analysis of the strategy and shenanigans in “Jet Lag: The Game” by three fans of the series. Every week during the season, we sit down to unpack strategic moves (and blunders), cheer on our favorite players, and play games about the game.

About

What?

Every typical episode of Baggage Claim maps directly onto a single episode of “Jet Lag: The Game”, but don’t worry if you haven’t watched it recently! We always start the episode with a recap, before getting into the nitty gritty details during our strategy discussion and a rotating segment about logistics. We don’t just stick to strategy, though, because Jet Lag wouldn’t be Jet Lag without The Game: so in every episode, we also pick a topic from the season at large and play a game about it.

When?

We cover each season as it comes out, with episodes releasing every Wednesday around the same time as the Jet Lag episode launch on Youtube. Between seasons airing, we cover old seasons!

Where should I start?

You can start with whichever season you want! This podcast began with Schengen Showdown, and we’ve covered every season since then (and we’re watching our way through the backlog during every offseason). So pick your favorite season of Jet Lag and delve right into our backlog.

Who are the hosts?

The three co-hosts of Baggage Claim are Casey (she/her), Jaya (they/them), and Kyra (she/her). They are three friends and fans of Jet Lag: The Game who simply decided enough was enough, and that Jet Lag needed a dedicated companion podcast that isn't behind a paywall. You can hear more about them in Episode 20: "Wait A Minute... Who ARE You?"
